MY JOURNEY

Education

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Journalism from Columbia University, where I specialized in investigative journalism. I also completed internships at renowned media organizations, honing my skills in reporting, writing, and interviewing. During my time at university, I actively participated in journalism clubs and organizations, furthering my knowledge and understanding of the field. I also took courses in media ethics and multimedia storytelling, which have shaped my approach to journalism. My education has provided me with a solid foundation in journalistic principles and practices, equipping me to pursue a successful career in journalism.

Career

I began my career as a staff writer at Hindustan Times, where I covered local politics and community issues. My in-depth reporting and engaging storytelling quickly earned me recognition, leading to a promotion to senior reporter. In my role as a senior reporter, I tackled complex investigative pieces, shedding light on corruption, environmental issues, and social injustices. My work was instrumental in bringing about positive change in the community. I'm currently retired with 40 years of experience in journalism.
